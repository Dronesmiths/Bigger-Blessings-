High-quality early education and subsidized care through the Family Child Care Home Education Network.

Who is it for?

Designed for families with children approx. 0-5 years old (until their 5th birthday) who qualify for subsidized care based on income (under 85% SMI) and need (working, seeking work, or vocational training). It is perfect for parents who want a home-like setting with school-level education.

What is included?

This is "more than just childcare." It is an educational program that includes DRDP (Desired Results Developmental Profile) assessments, nutritional meals, and parent-provider conferences to track your child's specific developmental progress.

Where does this happen?

In high-quality, licensed family child care homes that meet strict educational standards. Bigger Blessings is an active FCCHEN network partner.

When is it available?

Care is available during our standard operating hours, and often supports non-traditional schedules. Assessments are conducted regularly throughout the year to ensure your child is hitting key milestones.

Why choose FCCHEN?

Because early years are critical. FCCHEN ensures your child isn't just "watched" but is actively learning. You get the benefits of a small, nurturing home environment combined with the educational rigor of a large center.

How to Join the Network

Spaces in the FCCHEN program are limited and highly sought after.

1

Check Availability

Contact us directly to see if we have open FCCHEN spots for your child's age group. These spots are specific and separate from general enrollment.

Call Director: (661) 488-9737
2

Verify Eligibility

We work with the network administrators (often CCRC) to verify your income and need for care. You will need to provide proof of employment or school enrollment.

3

Enroll & Assess

Once approved, we will complete the enrollment packet and schedule your child's first developmental assessment to set their learning baseline.
