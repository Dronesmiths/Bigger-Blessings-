FCCHEN Enrollment

Quality early education and subsidized care through the Family Child Care Home Education Network.

More Than Just Childcare

As an FCCHEN provider, Bigger Blessings offers enhanced educational standards including DRDP assessments to track your child's growth.

1

Qualify for the Network

FCCHEN is designed for families who need subsidized care but want a high-quality educational environment. Eligibility is based on need and income.

2

Developmental Assessments

Enrolling through FCCHEN means your child receives regular DRDP (Desired Results Developmental Profile) assessments. We track their progress in:

  • Language & Literacy
  • Social-Emotional Development
  • Cognitive Skills
